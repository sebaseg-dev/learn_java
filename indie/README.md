# indie

## Purpose

This project is a way for me to apply what I’ve learned through a small, practical application.

As a developer, there are many important choices to make — for example, whether to work as an employee or as an independent professional. In France, choosing independence also means dealing with additional decisions and constraints, such as legal status and tax regimes.

As a former finance manager, I have experience reading and understanding tax regulations. With this project, I want to put that background to good use by creating a simple, open, and free tool that helps people understand the consequences of these choices.
